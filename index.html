About Us

My name is Đỗ Ngọc Quang Luân (bugkodc), and I am currently a dedicated third-year student at the University of Science and Education, the University of Da Nang.

With one year of experience in game programming, I have knowledge and skills in game development, game design, and level design.

My ultimate career goal is to become a Game Developer. My strong passion for game development started when I was in 9th grade. Despite being judged as someone who was only wasting time and lacked practicality, I remained determined to follow my chosen path.

I constantly strive to improve my skills and knowledge in this field. I believe that my creativity, teamwork abilities, and logical thinking will contribute to building amazing games.
